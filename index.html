4DFWD Deformation Analysis

Student Researcher

Investigated whether the Adidas 4DFWD running shoe delivers on its claim of converting vertical impact forces into forward horizontal propulsion. Designed the full experimental rig — camera placement, lighting stands, DIC masking — and processed deformation data in MATLAB to quantify midsole vector rotation vs. a control shoe (Ultraboost).

→ 14% greater horizontal vector rotation vs. Ultraboost — strongly validating Adidas' performance claim.

Personal Project

Designed and built a modular, 3D-printed laptop cooling stand with integrated axial fans for active convective cooling.
